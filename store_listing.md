## Full Description (use this in the "Detailed description" field)

**Take back your focus. Block the sites that steal your time.**

Focus Guard is a free, no-nonsense productivity extension that blocks distracting websites while you work. No subscriptions. No session limits. No account required.

---

**🚫 Blocklist Mode**
Add any sites you want blocked — Reddit, Twitter, YouTube, news sites — and they'll redirect to a focus reminder for the duration of your session.

**✅ Allowlist Mode**
Flip it around: block the entire internet except the sites you actually need. Perfect for deep work and research sessions.

**⏱ Timed Sessions**
Choose from 5, 10, 15, 30, 45, or 60-minute sessions. A countdown timer keeps you honest, and a distraction counter shows you exactly how many times you tried to stray.

**📊 Stats & Streaks**
Track your total focus time, weekly sessions, day streak, and a 7-day bar chart. See your Top 10 Most Visited Blocked Sites — so you know exactly which habits to break.

**⏰ Schedule Mode**
Set a daily focus session to start automatically at the same time every day. Great for morning routines and deep work blocks.

**🔒 100% Private**
Everything is stored locally on your device. No data is sent anywhere. No analytics. No tracking. Ever.

---

**How to use:**
1. Click the Focus Guard icon
2. Add the sites you want to block (or allow)
3. Pick a duration and hit Start
4. Work without distractions

That's it.
